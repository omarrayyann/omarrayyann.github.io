LEBSI

Nov 2021 - Active

Download

LEBSI enables people and clothing brands to set up online stores in minutes. Hence, sellers no longer have to manage their stores across multiple social media platforms at the same time. It provides physical stores with virtual marketplaces and allows them to expose themselves to potential customers, enabling them to compete with online brands.

  • Reached #22 on the App Store Top Charts and is currently attracting sellers from various regions around Jordan and the UAE.
  • Developed using Swift Language and uses Firebase Firestore Database to manage storage.
  • Finalist at the 2022 startAD NYUAD InnoVention Program - Pitch Deck
