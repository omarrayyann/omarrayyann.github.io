LEBSI

Nov 2021 - Active

Download

A platform that allows people and clothing brands to set up online stores and list their clothing pieces online in minutes. It prevents sellers from having to manage their stores on multiple social media platforms at the same time. It aims to help physical stores compete with online brands by providing an online marketplace for them to list their items on.

  • Reached #22 on the App Store Top Charts and is currently attracting sellers from various regions around Jordan
  • Developed using Swift Language and uses Firebase Firestore Database to manage storage.
  • Finalist at the 2022 NYUAD StartAD InnoVention Program - Pitch Deck
