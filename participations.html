NYUAD 10th Hackathon for Social Good in the Arab World

March 30 - April 1, 2022

Being my first non-remote hackathon, NYUAD 10th Hackathon for Social Good in the Arab World has set the bar high for my future ones. My team and I set out to find a problem that traditional computers can't solve efficiently and have it built on the Amazon brackets that qBraid provided us with. During the three-day hackathon, we worked on a project titled DelphiMed, which uses a Quantum Annealing optimization algorithm to decide which hospitals to assign patients to during a pandemic.
