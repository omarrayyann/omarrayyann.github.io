KATIB

Feb 2022 - Active

A handwriting assisting platform that provides contact-less kinesthetic feedback by utilizing magnetic forces. KATIB aims to help post-stroke patients regain writing skills throguh a user-friendly software that patients can carry out physical tasks through while getting direct evaluation by their therapist.

LEBSI

Nov 2021 - Active

LEBSI is a platform that allows people and clothing brands to set up online stores in minutes. It prevents sellers from having to manage their stores on multiple social media platforms at the same time. It helps physical stores compete with online brands by providing them with a virtual marketplace and potential audience.

A Fine Day

Aug 2021 - Jan 2022

A Fine Day is a gamified version of post-stroke rehabilitation therapy that involves the use of VR and hand-tracking technologies to help patients stick to their home exercise programs. It aims to improve the motor skills and range-of-motion of post-stroke patients while providing useful progress feedback.

Qatra

Apr 2021 - Aug 2021

Qatra is a platform that connects those who are in need of blood units in Jordan with potential blood donors. It allows user to join in by inputing their blood type and place of residence. Registered users would then start getting notifications once any local or surrounding hospital request blood units similar to their blood type.
