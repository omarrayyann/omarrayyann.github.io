Projects

LEBSI

April, 2021

LEBSI is a platform that allows people and clothing brands to set up online stores and list their clothing pieces in minutes. It prevents sellers from having to manage their stores on multiple social media platforms at the same time. LEBSI aims to help physical stores compete with online brands by providing an online marketplace for them to list their items on.
