Research

I am a PhD student at UCLA affiliated with the Robot Intelligence Lab (URIL). I do research on robot learning with a focus on imitation learning and sim2real. My goal is to build generally intelligent robots for everyday use.

Previously, I did my undergrad at NYU, where I was advised by Lerrel Pinto and Animesh Garg. In previous summers, I interned at Google.
