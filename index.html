Research

I am a PhD student at UCLA researching the intersection of machine learning and robotics, with a focus on imitation learning and sim2real. My goal is to develop general-purpose minds for robots that can help us in everyday life.

I was previously at NYU, advised by Lerrel Pinto and Animesh Garg. In previous summers, I interned at Google.
